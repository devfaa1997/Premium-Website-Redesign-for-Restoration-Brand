Restoration & Mitigation

Our water damage mitigation process blends cutting-edge technology with strict IICRC S500 standards to ensure not just dryness, but truly restored environmental health.

  • ✓ Precision Assessment: Utilizing advanced moisture meters and thermal imaging to map all affected areas, ensuring no hidden moisture is overlooked.
  • ✓ Surgical Demolition: We remove only compromised drywall and insulation, preserving structural integrity and minimizing repair costs.
  • ✓ Strategic Drying: We engineer a targeted drying system using industrial-grade air movers and dehumidifiers to reduce moisture levels to industry-approved targets.
  • ✓ Validation & Verification: Through continuous daily monitoring and documentation, we ensure complete drying is achieved. We then engage independent, certified hygienists to provide third-party verification.
